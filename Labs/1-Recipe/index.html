Cassava leaves Recipe

Welcome to my new recipe I want to build tohether. It all about Cassava Leaves for 4 persons.

Ingredients

  • 1 brazier with charcoal or 1 gas stove.
    • 1 Pestle and 1 mortar.
  • 1 kg of sorted cassava leaves.
  • 1/2 liter of palm oil.
  • 3 liters of water.
  • 1 onion.
  • 2 leeks.
  • 2 green bell peppers.

Instructions

  1. Wash the cassava leaves thoroughly with warm water.
  2. Pound the cassava leaves.
  3. Place the cassava leaves in a pot.
  4. Add the two chopped bell peppers.
  5. Add the two chopped leeks.
  6. Put in the palm oil.
  7. Let it boil for 2 hours until fully cooked.
